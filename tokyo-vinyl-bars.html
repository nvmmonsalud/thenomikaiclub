Listening rooms to bookmark

Each spot comes with house rules and the best time to visit.

Bar Martha — Ebisu

Low-lit, soulful, and precise. No talking near the bar; whispered conversations only. Highball perfection.

Music charge: ~¥1,100 Cash only

JBS — Shibuya

Thousands of records curated by owner Kobayashi-san. Genre changes nightly; expect deep jazz cuts.

No table charge Beer & whisky only

Grandfather's — Shinjuku

Stevie Wonder on repeat, warm wood interiors, and a dance-friendly energy on weekends.

Cover: ~¥1,000 after 9pm Vinyl requests welcome

Studio Mule — Omotesando

Modern listening room run by Mule Musiq. Top-tier sound system, cocktail program, and rotating DJs.

Reservations advised Genre: eclectic
