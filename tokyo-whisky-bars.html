How we choose

Small, conversation-first rooms with thoughtful glassware, careful ice, and bartenders who share notes without pretense.

¥¥-¥¥¥ Table charges noted English-friendly when available
Ask for a flight

Let bartenders pour side-by-sides of Chichibu, Mars, or Yoichi to learn the differences in a single sitting.

Pair with snacks

Try smoked nuts, chocolate, or dried fruits; most bars keep small plates that amplify the dram.

Reservations and cash notes included where needed.

Legendary bar with shelves of Scotch oddities and impeccably clear ice. Owner seats are limited; come early on weekdays.

Independent bottlings, hand-cut ice, and great conversation about Japanese distilleries. Easy walk from Shinsen Station.

No reservations English OK
