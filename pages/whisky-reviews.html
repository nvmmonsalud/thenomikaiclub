Suntory

Yamazaki 18 Year

Dark fruit, leather, and sherry-soaked oak with a long, luxurious finish.

  • Nose: Raisin, dried cherry, cedar, and espresso
  • Palate: Bitter chocolate, clove, toasted oak
  • Finish: Lingering spice, sandalwood warmth

Rating: 95/100

Suntory

Hibiki 30 Year

Silken blend of aged malt and grain with honeyed florals and incense.

  • Nose: Plum blossom, honeycomb, antique wood
  • Palate: Dried apricot, orange marmalade, soft peat
  • Finish: Long, perfumed, meditative

Rating: 97/100

Suntory

Hakushu 18 Year

Forest-fresh malt with green herbs, citrus peel, and wisps of smoke.

  • Nose: Pine needles, yuzu, mint
  • Palate: Pear, white pepper, gentle smoke
  • Finish: Crisp, mineral, cool smoke trail

Rating: 94/100
