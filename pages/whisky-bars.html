Map & neighborhood hints

Ginza / Yurakucho

Search above department stores and along backstreets near Sukiyabashi for speakeasy-style counters.

Shinjuku Golden Gai

Use the narrow alleys; many whisky bars sit on the second floor with small neon plaques.

Asakusa

Retro lounges cluster around Kappabashi Street—look for wood signs and single malt banners.

Ebisu

Basements near Ebisu Station often hide intimate whisky libraries with English-friendly menus.
