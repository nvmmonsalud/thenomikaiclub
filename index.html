Map & neighborhood hints

Ginza

Find bar clusters tucked above street level along Namiki-dori and showrooms near Yurakucho.

Shinjuku

Golden Gai’s narrow alleys hide tiny whisky counters and vinyl dens with themed playlists.

Shibuya

Walk from Dogenzaka to Kamiyamacho for audiophile bars with rotary mixers and vintage JBLs.

Ebisu

Cozy basements around Ebisu Garden Place pair rare single malts with soulful jazz nights.
