Single Grain · Miyagi

Nikka Coffey Grain

Region: Miyagi Distillery: Miyagikyo (Coffey Still) Cask: Refill & New Oak ABV: 45% Age: NAS
★ 8.3 / 10

Overview

A silky, dessert-forward grain whisky that highlights Nikka's historic Coffey still with creamy citrus and toffee sweetness.

Tasting notes

Nose

Lemon curd, vanilla wafer, banana cream, and soft baking spice.

Palate

Silky custard, orange oils, toffee, and a touch of coconut flake.

Finish

Medium with crème brûlée crust, oak char, and a little mocha bitterness.

Pairing suggestions

Butter mochi, taiyaki with custard, or roasted cashews to echo the creamy sweetness.
