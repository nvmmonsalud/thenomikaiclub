Blend · Saitama

Chichibu Ichiro's Malt & Grain

Region: Saitama Distillery: Chichibu & Friends Cask: Ex-Bourbon & Wine ABV: 46.5% Age: NAS
★ 8.6 / 10

Overview

A vibrant world blend anchored by Chichibu malt, offering tropical brightness and a playful, malty chew.

Tasting notes

Nose

Pineapple gummies, green apple peel, vanilla wafer, and a brush of white pepper.

Palate

Mango sorbet, honeycomb, airy malt biscuit, and a splash of lemon soda.

Finish

Medium-long with toasted oak, cocoa nib, and lingering tropical sweetness.

Pairing suggestions

Citrus pound cake, karaage with lemon, or a plate of fresh cut pineapple.
