Single Malt · Kansai

Yamazaki 12 Year

Region: Kansai Distillery: Yamazaki Cask: American, Sherry & Mizunara Oak ABV: 43% Age: 12 years
★ 8.8 / 10

Overview

A benchmark Japanese single malt that balances orchard fruit, soft Mizunara incense, and honeyed malt into an elegant daily sipper.

Tasting notes

Nose

Golden apples, apricot, acacia honey, and a hint of sandalwood incense.

Palate

Bright peach, vanilla custard, toasted barley, and gentle spice from Mizunara oak.

Finish

Medium length with citrus oils, white pepper, and lingering sweet oak.

Pairing suggestions

Grilled yakitori with tare, candied ginger, or a buttery shortbread to echo the malt sweetness.
